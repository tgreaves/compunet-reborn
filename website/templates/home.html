COMPUNET REBORN

THE LIVE ONE LIVES ON

Compunet was a UK-based interactive online service that ran from 1984 to 1993, serving Commodore 64 users. It featured user-generated content, electronic mail, telesoftware downloads, and a unique community experience.

Compunet Reborn is an effort to reverse engineer and recreate the original protocol, bringing this piece of computing history back to life on modern systems.

FEATURES

WHAT WORKS TODAY

Directory browsing, content viewing, program downloads and uploads, electronic mail (Courier), user-generated content uploads, voting, and the full duckshoot menu system.

GET CONNECTED

All you need is VICE (C64 emulator) with SwiftLink enabled. No modem emulator required — connect directly to the server.
