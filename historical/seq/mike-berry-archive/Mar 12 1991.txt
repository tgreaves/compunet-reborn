552617  CNET REPLY and RUBBISH answers
some of BOXX's criticisms of the net.
The whole point is that, unlike any
other net, this one is NOT "managed" by
them upstairs. It's managed mainly by
US, the users. Our Editor does what he
can, and what we tell him about or ask
him for (usually). It's no good mailing
Nick Green: he's a director not an
operative. Send your mail to FEEDBACK
about net management & usage, to
HOTLINE about tech matters, & to ADMIN
about accounts. That's the official
method, anyway. If you've a friend at
court you murmur in his earhole
(nicely).  I've been known to whinge
about the net myself at times - when I
wear my other hat with the red tassel.
