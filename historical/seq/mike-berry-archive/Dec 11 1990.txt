 Here's an intro to start the ball
rolling while I look at the overnight
uploads. Hope it amuses you: it's true.

 As I was leaving work today a lady came
up to me in the car park & asked if I
could back her car out. Someone had
parked about 2" from her. "I can't seem
to manage it", she said:-  which wasn't
surprising as her front wheels were at
45 degrees angle. I edged forward & back
& then asked her "Are the wheels
straight now?"  She looked at the near-
side front wheel & said, "well this one
is" and then she went round & looked at
the other one! "I think that one is",
she said!  Seems she was about right, as
I got out easily.             
                  Sheesh!!          Gn.
