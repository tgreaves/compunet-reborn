 3) How (roughly) does Cnet error
checking/protocol work?

Data is divided into packets, with a
header including

 start of packet byte (1)
 packet length
 CRC
 Packet type
 data
 end of packet byte (2)

 each packet is sent in a 4-window
 system. eg packets 40,41,42,43 are
 sent consectutively, and 40 must be
 ACKed before the window moves along to
 ramge 41-44
