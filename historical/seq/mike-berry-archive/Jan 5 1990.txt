 FASTRAK:
We had a minor panic on Wednesday night
when Fastrak informed us that we
wouldn't be able to use their network
after all. Nick Green went home rather
sick (to say the least) and didn't
exactly enjoy a peaceful nights sleep.
The last thing we wanted was to have
to look to other networks and start
from scratch yet again.

Thankfully, the news was much better on
Thursday morning. Fastrak ARE able to
accommodate our system and all the
preparations are now going ahead.
One of their main worries was that we
would overload their network with the
sheer volume of data being transfered at
one time. They feared we might block
their network during the day too (!!),
clearly we have a lot to learn about
each other.

It is hoped that the first online tests
will start within two to three weeks.
Fastrak want to do tests on this new
service that we are bringing to their
network and that means free connect time
via your new local numbers for a period
when the service is first made
available.

Ofcourse we will list all the new
numbers as soon as we have a start date.
(The  C  have already listed most of the
numbers in their news area).

   Dral
