      HAPPY NEW YEAR TO YOU ALL!

 Since Mercury telecom have cheap rates
all day today, I'll try to cover a few
extra items over the past week...on
nights when there was no review. Can't
promise though.

 Here's a funny to start you off. I
went into Power Computing on Monday and
asked "Have you a copy of the book
Inside the Amiga with C?". And a rather
tired looking lad behind the counter
asked, "Is it for the Amiga or the ST?"

Still on WED we had one of Dicey's
exotic frame titles HAIRY TESTICLES. If
you remember 1988-89 on Cnet, Dicey did
a very colourful review each week, and
