Ian has added some very useful frames
for Graham's (GW9) Amiga Cnet
software Vn3.14 . These frames tell you
all you need to know about editing on
the miggy, including the graphic
characters available. Some people don't
perhaps know that you can produce all
the C64 graphics on the miggy, but you
probably need a chart. With the new
EDITASSIST you can have this chart
beside you in another window, & just
click on the character you want to
transfer into your edit frame. Many
thanks Graham for another piece if
wizardry.
