 Well it's nice to be back at the old
desk again. The place has changed a
bit since November '89 & I hardly
had time to look around last week. I
see Ken and Aidan have cleaned the
floor & scrubbed the graffiti off the
walls. There's a new fridge (I'll keep
my whipping cream in that): and
Bonzo's kennel in the corner has had a
lick of paint. The waste-basket is
full of discarded logos from Gizmo &
Zoggo, and there's a framed Dicey logo
on the wall captioned "This is how it
should be done."  And TJ's very special
basket chair with the hole in the seat
still sags when one sits in it. I
suppose that's TJ's camp bed over there.
        Oh well: on with the dance.
