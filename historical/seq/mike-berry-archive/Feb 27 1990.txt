    COMPUNET TODAY:  27th FEBRUARY 1990

 Well, as I mentioned earlier in  TUE
 Review , Nick Green went up to
Peterborough this afternoon to talk to
the big-wigs at Fastrak. At 9pm he rang
me sounding tired and not over joyous.

He had hoped to bring the necessary
equipment back with him which would
allow us to start tests at the Compunet
end of the line. Unfortunately this was
not possible.

After much discussion, Fastrak have
promised to have the equipment to us
within a few days and also confirm the
date from which full scale tests can go
ahead.

We are sorry it is not brighter news, we
are somewhat relieved to be able to give
you ANY news after the speed at which
things have been progressing.   Peter
